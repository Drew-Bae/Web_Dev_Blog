Welcome to my first blog post! My name is Drew Bae and I am an aspiring web developer. My goal is to keep you updated with the latest trends and techniques in web development through my comprehensive blog. I will be sharing relevant codes and links to aid you on your web development journey. I hope you will subscribe and join me on this exciting journey.

The topic of this post is Artificial Intelligence (AI). It's 2023, and AI has captured everyone's attention, especially the well-known AI model, ChatGPT. Although I am not an expert in the field of AI, I can clearly see its significance. When I first heard about AI's capabilities, I was skeptical, but I decided to try it out for myself. I created an account to access ChatGPT and was astounded by its capabilities.

At the time, I was working on a personal project and had been struggling with a problem for two days. I decided to ask ChatGPT for help, and to my surprise, the AI model solved the problem in just 15 seconds. The question was, "How do I make a video a background using CSS?" The fact that an AI model could solve a problem that had taken me two days was both amazing and a little disheartening. I had just earned a B.S. degree in Computer Science that took me four years, and now I was wondering if AI was going to replace me.

However, after some research and reflection, I realized that AI is not here to replace us, but to assist us. AI has the ability to process and analyze data at a much faster pace than humans, freeing us up to focus on higher-level tasks. In web development, AI can automate tedious tasks, improve user experience, and provide valuable insights and solutions.

Take this blog as an example. It was designed entirely by AI! If you take a look at my post, you can find the design created by MidJourney at the bottom. I made some modifications for optimization purposes. Also, if you click the button, it will guide you on how to do the same.

As a web developer, it's essential to stay informed about the latest advancements in AI and see how it can enhance our skills. Embracing change and utilizing AI to our advantage will make us stronger web developers.

In conclusion, AI is here to support us, not replace us. As web developers, we should stay informed and embrace the changes that AI brings to our field. I hope this post has given you a glimpse into the world of AI and its relation to web development. If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to leave them in the comments section. Thank you for reading, and I look forward to sharing more with you in my future blog posts!
